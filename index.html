About

The Mercedes-Benz Museum in Stuttgart celebrates the automobile invented by Carl Benz in 1886:
it relates its history and tells its stories, bringing both alive by placing them in the
context of technology, day-to-day life, social history and popular culture.
More than 160 vehicles of all types are the main protagonists.


They range from some of the oldest automobiles ever built ot legendary racing cars and

futuristic research vehicles.


Together with other exhibits, they form the centerpiece of the permanent exhibition
convering a total of 16.500 square meters in twelve rooms. This unparalleled world can Benz
discovered on two hours that follow a "Legend" and "Collection" narrative.
